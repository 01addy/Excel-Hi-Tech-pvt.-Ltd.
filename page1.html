EXCEL HI-TECH PRIVATE LIMITED

Excel Hi-tech Private Limited is a Private incorporated on 26 May 1997. It is classified as Non-govt company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Delhi. Its authorized share capital is Rs. 11,500,000 and its paid up capital is Rs. 9,368,500.

Excel Hi-tech Private Limited's Annual General Meeting (AGM) was last held on N/A and as per records from Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), its balance sheet was last filed on 31 March 2022.

Directors of Excel Hi-tech Private Limited are Rajat Maheshwari, Umesh Nath Maheshwari, Pooja Maheshwari and Veena Maheshwari.

Manufacturers of nickel and cobalt base alloy castings monel, cz100, hastelloy, inconel, stellite
excel hi tech is situated at ghaziabad, famous for foundries and machine shop close to the capital of india, new delhi, by just 25 km well connected by roads, air-route in an area called ncr national capital region.


Having been into commercial production since 1998, today in a span of few year, endowed with state of the art and technology backed with continuous research and development company stands as a specialized foundry for the supply and manufacturing of nickel and cobalt base alloys, both static and centrifugal, corrosion, heat and wear resistant casting with a vertical integration to deliver fully machined or proof machine components.

The company is headed by mr. Umesh n. Maheshwari, a mechanical engineer with 30 years of experience in foundry and metal industry. The company day to day affairs and over all management is looked by mr. Rajat maheshwari, a mechanical engineer with 12 years of experience in same group industry.
The companies mission is to be a low cost profitable integrated casting product unit, converting resources into competitive and innovative quality product for customers.

Infrastructure

The company has set up facilities to manufacture casting up to 350 kg as a single piece, equipped with all testing machines.
